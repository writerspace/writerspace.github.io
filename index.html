writer space

by c.e. hermes

Made with Creative Commons music and videos, WriterSpace is meant to be a solace for those who need a place to think.

These days, distractions are everywhere. It's getting harder to separate ourselves from the outside world and listen to our inner minds. As a writer, I created WriterSpace because I needed a peaceful environment for my work - one where I could escape.

I hope you enjoy this space as much as I do. Your work and personal settings are saved in local browser storage, so you can come back to edit anytime. Just in case, be sure to save a backup copy as well.

Thanks for stopping by.
